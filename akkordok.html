Chords — Module Guide

How It Works

16 chords appear on the staff. Hold all the correct notes of each chord simultaneously on the piano. Release to advance to the next chord. Wrong notes are highlighted in red.

Controls
Mode ▲▼Notes (sheet only), Chords (name + notes after solving), Roman (numeral).
Level ▲▼Root position → Inversions → 7th chords.
Top / BottomToggle treble and bass clef chord generation independently.
HintsShows individual note names within each chord and activates LED guides.
Learning Tip

In Roman mode, the chord name is hidden until you solve it — great for training functional harmony recognition.
