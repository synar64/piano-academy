Scales — Module Guide

How It Works

Select a scale and press Start. The notes appear on the staff — play them in order on the piano. The active note is highlighted. Complete the scale up and down to finish the round.

Controls
Scale ▲▼Cycle through all 24 major and minor scales.
Mode ▲▼Sprint (1×), Marathon (3×), Training (endless loop).
Octave ▲▼Switch between 1 and 2 octave scale runs.
HintsShows note names and activates the LED light guide on the keyboard.
FingersDisplays the official scale fingering (1–5) on the sheet.
WheelOpens the Circle of Fifths. Click any major key sector to immediately load that scale.
Learning Tip

Start with Sprint mode and 1 octave. Once you can play a scale cleanly, switch to 2 octaves and Marathon to build muscle memory.

Circle of Fifths

Press Wheel to open the draggable Circle of Fifths. Click any sector to instantly load that major scale — the OLED Scale display updates automatically. Keys arranged by harmonic distance: adjacent keys share 6 out of 7 notes, making scale transitions smooth. Drag the wheel anywhere on screen.
