Sight Read — Module Guide

How It Works

16 notes appear on the staff one at a time. Play the correct note on the piano as quickly as possible. Wrong notes are highlighted in red — keep trying until you hit the right one.

Controls
Top / BottomToggle Treble clef (C4–G5) and Bass clef (F2–C4) note pools.
HintsShows the note name next to each notehead on the staff.
KeysLights up the target key on the piano keyboard with a blue LED.
Mode ▲▼Session: one round then results. Flow: endless auto-continue.
Level ▲▼Diatonic (white keys) → Rhythm (note values) → Chromatic (all keys).
Learning Tip

Use Hints to learn note names on the staff, and Keys to learn their position on the keyboard — but try not to use both at once, as the real skill is connecting the two yourself.
