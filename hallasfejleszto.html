Ear Train — Module Guide

How It Works

Eight notes appear on the staff as ? symbols. The synthesizer plays each note — your task is to identify it and press the correct key on the piano. The faster and more accurately you respond, the higher your score.

Feedback System
CORRECTThe note lights up green on the staff and the exercise advances automatically.
HIGHER ↑Your note was too low — the target is higher on the keyboard.
LOWER ↓Your note was too high — the target is lower on the keyboard.
TIME UPTimer expired. The note is revealed in red and counts as a miss.
Controls
Top / BottomToggle Treble clef (C4–G5) and Bass clef (F2–C4) note pools.
ChromaticAdds black key notes (sharps/flats) to the pool — significantly harder.
HintsShows the note name on the staff before you play it. Great for learning.
ReplayReplays the current note's sound without penalty.
Timer ▲▼Adjust seconds per note (3–15s). Cannot be changed during a session.
Progression

Score 100% on a round to automatically drop the timer by one step — making the next round harder. Reach 3 seconds per note for maximum difficulty.
