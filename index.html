Akíra Piano Academy

Browser-Based Piano Training Workstation

About

A comprehensive, free, browser-based piano training environment. Six independent modules cover every essential area of piano education — from ear training and sight-reading to chords, scales, technique, and rhythm. Works with a real MIDI keyboard or with mouse clicks on the on-screen piano. No installation required.

Modules

Ear TrainingIdentify individual notes and intervals by ear across multiple octave ranges. Includes chromatic option for black keys.
Sight ReadingRead notes from the staff and play them in real time. BPM and mistake tracking, hint system, LED keyboard guides.
ChordsRecognize and play diatonic chords, inversions, and seventh chords. Includes a draggable Circle of Fifths wheel showing key relationships.
ScalesAll 24 major and minor scales with official fingering, LED keyboard guides, and Circle of Fifths navigation for key selection.
HanonClassic Hanon finger exercises with real-time BPM and stability tracking. Identifies which finger causes timing slowdowns.
RhythmRead and tap rhythmic patterns. Three modes: READ (follow the score), TAP (free tapping with accuracy bar), COPY (listen then repeat). 12 patterns across 4 levels.

How to Use

Connect a MIDI keyboard before opening a module for the best experience. All modules remember your language preference. Press the ? button in any module and click any element to learn what it does. Your language choice is saved automatically across all modules.

Technical

Runs entirely in the browser — no server, no login, no data collection. Audio engine uses the Web Audio API with real Steinway Grand Piano samples (Salamander). MIDI input via Web MIDI API. Best experienced in Chrome or Edge.
