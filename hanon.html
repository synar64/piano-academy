How It Works

Classic Hanon finger exercises displayed on the staff. Play each note in sequence — the exercise scrolls through positions automatically. BPM and stability are tracked in real time.

Controls

Exercise ▲▼Select the Hanon exercise number.
Top / BottomToggle treble and bass clef note generation independently.
HintsDisplays note names above each notehead.
FingersDisplays official fingering numbers (1–5) below noteheads.
Rhythm ▲▼Select a rhythmic pattern for the exercise.
Learning Tip

After the session, the analysis shows which finger caused the most slowdowns. Focus on lifting that finger higher before each keystroke and keep your wrist relaxed.
