Rhythm — Module Guide

How It Works

Rhythmic patterns are displayed on the staff as note symbols. In READ mode, play along with the metronome. In TAP mode, tap any key to the beat — the system measures your timing accuracy in real time. In COPY mode, listen first, then repeat.

Controls
Mode ▲▼Tap the rhythm on every beat shown on the score. Use spacebar, MIDI keyboard, or click the piano keys.
Pattern ▲▼Select from 12 rhythmic patterns across 4 difficulty levels.
Tempo ▲▼Adjust BPM from 40 to 200. Click the Tempo cell to reset to 80.
MetroToggle the metronome click. Beat 1 is accented.
LoopLoop the pattern continuously instead of stopping after one pass.
Learning Tip

Start slow — set the tempo well below your comfort zone. Accuracy at 60 BPM is worth more than sloppy playing at 120. The green accuracy bar shows how well your taps align with the beat grid.
